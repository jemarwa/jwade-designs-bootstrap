About

Jennifer is the name. AKA jwade. During the week, I'm known as a software engineer. I've worked with various technologies to get the job done, most recently Java, React, and Redux. Leading the team to greatness one day at a time.

In my spare time, doodling is my game. Mostly customized greeting cards for any occasion. Creating my own alphabet of animals book is something too in which I have dabbled. Ultimately I'd like to provide cute prints that can be enjoyed by all!
